About UnityMUN

UnityMUN is a Model United Nations conference dedicated to empowering students through diplomacy, debate, and collaboration. Delegates represent countries and organizations to discuss pressing global challenges while developing leadership and public speaking skills.

UnityMUN 2025/26 will bring together passionate students for a premium, professional, and engaging MUN experience.
